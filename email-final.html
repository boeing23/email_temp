Unmasking Screenplays

 

Cinemind is a story and screenplay analysis platform. It uses AI to analyze a story, screenplay, book/manuscript or a TV/series pilot episode to generate a 5-8 page report that gives you a logline, a detailed synopsis, character summaries, SWOT analysis, conclusion and various other data points that help you objectively analyze an IP. And it does so in less than 3 minutes.

 

Cinemind can be customized to add more specific data points according to the needs of the user like how the material fits into your company slate or identify similar movies in the past along with their box office numbers, etc. It is very easy to use, just sign up and begin. For details, please check cinemind.co
